Music Rateur

This site was designed and published as part of the COMP 333 Software Engineering class at Wesleyan University. The described platform is not publicly available but rather is created as a training exercise.

Features:

  • You can rate and review music!
  • You can discuss music with other users!
  • Our advanced AI attunes music taste to you and creates a PERSONALIZED playlist!

Pricing Tiers:

  1. For free: you can rate, review, and use our forums to discuss music.
  2. For the low cost of 0.99 a month: you have access to our state-of-the-art attuning AI!
